THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Every B2B company in SA needs a predictable pipeline of new business meetings. Most cannot afford a full-time Business Development Manager (R30,000–R60,000/month). You build an AI-powered cold outreach system — scrape leads, enrich data with AI, write hyper-personalized email and LinkedIn sequences, and book meetings directly into the client's calendar. You charge per meeting booked. Your cost per meeting is R80–150 (AI + time). You charge R500–R1,500 per booked meeting. The margin is exceptional, and performance pricing makes it an easy sell.
