THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

As autonomous AI agents proliferate, businesses face a critical problem: which agents can they trust? Just as Glassdoor transformed hiring by making company culture transparent, "Glassdoor for AI Agents" becomes the authoritative source for AI agent reviews, performance benchmarks, reliability scores, and compliance ratings. Businesses use it to evaluate agents before deploying them. Developers use it to improve their agents. Enterprises use it for vendor due diligence. This is a 2–5 year build that becomes increasingly valuable as the agent economy grows.
