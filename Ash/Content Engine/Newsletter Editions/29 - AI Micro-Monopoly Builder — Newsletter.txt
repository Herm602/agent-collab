A Micro-Monopoly is a business that dominates a very small, specific niche so completely that competition becomes irrelevant. "The only AI tool for SA plumbers" or "The only WhatsApp bot builder for SA churches." You systematically identify underserved SA niches, build AI-powered tools or services for each, achieve dominant market share, and then maintain with minimal ongoing effort.
