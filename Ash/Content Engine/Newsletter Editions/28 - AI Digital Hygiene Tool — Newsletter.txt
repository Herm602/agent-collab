THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

As AI scrapes, analyzes, and weaponizes personal data at unprecedented scale, individuals and businesses need a comprehensive tool to audit, manage, and clean their digital footprint. The AI Digital Hygiene Tool is a personal and business data management platform: it scans the internet for your data, identifies risks, sends takedown requests, monitors for new exposures, and provides an ongoing privacy health score. In SA, POPIA gives individuals the right to have their data removed — but no tool currently helps them exercise this right at scale.
