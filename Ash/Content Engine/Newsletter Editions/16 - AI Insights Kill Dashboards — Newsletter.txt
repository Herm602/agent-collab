THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SA executives are drowning in data but starving for insight. Monthly management reports are 30-page PDFs nobody reads. Dashboards show numbers but answer no questions. The solution is an AI-powered "Decision Intelligence" layer: instead of a dashboard, the executive asks "Why did we miss target in Cape Town this month?" and the AI finds the answer in seconds — pulling from sales data, operations, HR, and external market data simultaneously. You build this for mid-to-large SA companies as a managed analytics service, replacing expensive BI consultants and data scientists.
