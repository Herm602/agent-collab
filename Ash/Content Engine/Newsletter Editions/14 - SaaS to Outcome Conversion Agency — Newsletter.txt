SA companies collectively pay hundreds of millions of rands annually for SaaS tools they barely use — HubSpot, Salesforce, Monday.com, Slack, Notion, and more. Studies show 68% of SaaS licenses are underutilized. Your agency specializes in taking SaaS tools companies already own and maximizing their ROI using AI automation and workflow design. You get paid a consulting fee PLUS a share of the measurable revenue uplift or cost saving. This is a high-trust, high-margin model with excellent retention.
