Content consistency is the #1 marketing challenge for SA small businesses. Most SME owners know they should be posting daily on Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn — but they don't have the time, skill, or budget for a full-time social media manager (R12,000–R20,000/month). You use AI to generate an entire month of branded social media content in 2 hours per client, then schedule it using Buffer or Later, and charge R3,000–R8,000/month. Instagram has 14M+ SA users; Facebook has 24M+ — both growing (We Are Social, 2025).
AI content tools (Jasper, Copy.ai) are growing 45% annually — market is validated.
SA digital marketing industry grew 22% in 2024 (DMASA). SME segment most underserved.
TikTok SA has 10M+ users — Reels/TikTok video content is the next frontier for this service.
Average SA agency charges R10,000–R25,000/month for social media — massive price gap for AI-powered providers.
