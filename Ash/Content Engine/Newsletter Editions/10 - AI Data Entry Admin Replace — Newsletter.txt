THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Admin and data entry are the largest avoidable costs in SA logistics, healthcare, retail, and professional services. A single data entry clerk costs R8,000–R15,000/month in salary, plus UIF, skills levy, and management overhead. AI-powered document processing (OCR + AI extraction + RPA) can replace 80% of repetitive admin tasks at a fraction of the cost. You build and manage these systems for companies, charging R12,000–R35,000/month — replacing 2–4 staff members while saving the client R20,000–R60,000/month net. The ROI sells itself.

WHY SOUTH AFRICA, WHY NOW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Logistics & freight: 15,000+ companies, 10,000+ documents/month each. Target: mid-size forwarders.
Medical billing companies: Processes 50,000+ claims monthly. Accuracy critical. High pain.
Law firms (conveyancing): FICA + bond documentation = 200+ pages per transfer.
Government suppliers: BEE compliance documentation, tender submissions, reporting.
Importers/exporters: Customs documentation, tariff codes, clearing agent communication.
