THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The most defensible AI businesses are those that own a single vertical so completely that competitors cannot displace them. A Vertical AI Company picks ONE industry — construction, agriculture, legal, healthcare, logistics — and builds every AI workflow, tool, and service that industry needs. You go so deep that switching costs become enormous. In SA, most industries are underserved by AI, and local compliance knowledge (NHBRC, SARS, FSCA, etc.) creates a natural moat. Target: R500K–R2M MRR within 3 years in a single vertical.
