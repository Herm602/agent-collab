THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

As AI becomes ubiquitous, cyberattacks are becoming AI-powered — faster, more targeted, and harder to detect. SA is one of the most attacked countries in the world (ranked 3rd highest cybercrime rate globally in 2024). Most SA SMEs and mid-size companies have no AI security posture whatsoever. You build an AI security consulting and monitoring service that audits businesses for AI-specific vulnerabilities, trains staff on AI threats (deepfakes, AI phishing), deploys AI-powered threat monitoring, and provides POPIA-compliant security frameworks. This is a premium, high-trust, high-retention bus
