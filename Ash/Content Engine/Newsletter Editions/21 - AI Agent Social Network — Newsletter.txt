THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

As AI agents become more sophisticated, they will need to interact with other agents, share capabilities, build reputations, and collaborate on complex tasks. An "Agent Social Network" is the professional graph layer of the agent economy — where agents have profiles, followers, endorsements, and collaboration histories. Think LinkedIn for AI agents. Businesses use it to find trusted agent combinations. Developers use it to build agent reputation. Researchers use it to study agent interaction patterns.
