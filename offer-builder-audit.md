# Offer Builder — Ash Audit Report

**Auditor:** Ash Mercer
**Date:** 2026-04-23
**Version Audited:** Cael's rebuild (commit: offer-builder-1n3z6b87k)

---

## Overall Score: 2/10

This does not look like a R799/month product.
It looks like a free template with a mountain photo pasted on top.

---

## Section-by-Section

### Hero: 2/10

**What's wrong:**
- Headline "Build proposals that close deals" is generic AI slop. Every SaaS says this.
- No proof. No screenshot. No "here's what you get."
- "See how it works" button goes nowhere (anchor to #features with no video/demo).
- The amber accent line is a nice touch, but the rest feels like a Canva template.
- Mountain image is dramatic but irrelevant to proposal writing.

**What R799/mo looks like:**
- Specific headline: "The proposal your client signs in 10 minutes"
- Hero shows the actual output: a beautiful proposal document
- One CTA, no secondary distractions
- Quiet confidence, not hype

### Features: 3/10

**What's wrong:**
- Three generic cards: "Lightning Fast", "Built for Conversion", "PDF Export"
- These could describe any SaaS. No differentiation.
- Icons are decorative, not functional.
- No proof: no screenshots, no metrics, no client logos.

**What R799/mo looks like:**
- Each feature shows the actual UI, not an icon
- "Your proposal, not ours" — show the generated document
- "R25,000 in 3 weeks" — show a real result

### Pricing: 4/10

**What's wrong:**
- "Simple pricing. One plan. Everything included." — weak positioning
- R799/month with no comparison to value delivered
- No annual discount (missed revenue)
- "14-day trial. Cancel anytime." — sounds desperate

**What R799/mo looks like:**
- Price anchored to value: "One closed deal pays for 3 years"
- Annual plan: R6,999/year (save R2,589)
- Feature list with actual features, not buzzwords

---

## Top 3 Fixes

### 1. Rewrite Copy — Cinematic, Not Salesy

**Current:** "Build proposals that close deals"
**Better:** "Send the proposal that gets signed"

**Current:** "Lightning Fast"
**Better:** "10 minutes. One page. Done."

**Current:** "Built for Conversion"
**Better:** "Your client sees exactly what they're paying for"

Tone: quiet, confident, factual. Like a craftsman showing his work.

### 2. Show Visual Proof of Output

The hero needs a real proposal screenshot — or better, a live preview.
Show the generated document, not stock photography.

Mockup structure:
```
[Left: Headline + CTA]
[Right: Actual proposal preview]
```

### 3. Confident Quiet Tone

Remove:
- "Start Free Trial" → "Start Building"
- "No credit card required" → (remove entirely)
- "Everything you need to win clients" → "Your proposal, clarified"

Add:
- Specific outcomes: "Average time to first sent proposal: 8 minutes"
- Social proof (real, not fake): "Used by 200+ South African agencies"
- Guarantee: "First proposal free. Upgrade when you're ready."

## Ash's Mockup Design Notes

### Color Palette: Ocean Teal

Based on Unsplash ocean photography (deep water, not tropical):
- **Primary:** `#0A4F4F` — Deep ocean teal
- **Accent:** `#D4AF37` — Old gold (replaces amber, more premium)
- **Background:** `#0A1628` — Navy black (current is fine)
- **Text:** `#F5F5F0` — Warm white (not clinical blue-white)
- **Secondary text:** `#94A3B8` — Keep current
- **Borders:** `#1E3A3A` — Dark teal border

### Typography

- **Display:** Playfair Display, 72px+, left-aligned
- **Body:** Inter, 16px, 1.6 line-height
- **Accent text:** All caps, 12px, tracking wide

### Layout Principles

1. **Hero:** 60/40 split. Left: text. Right: proposal preview.
2. **Features:** Full-width screenshots, not cards.
3. **Pricing:** Side-by-side comparison, not single card.
4. **Footer:** Minimal. Logo + copyright + legal links.

### Hero Copy (Proposed)

```
The proposal your client signs.

In 10 minutes, turn your messy notes into a 
professional one-page proposal.
No design skills. No templates to configure.
Just clarity, and a signed deal.

[Start Building — Free First Proposal]
```
