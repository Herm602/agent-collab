THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

As AI generates more of the world's content, products, and decisions, there will be an explosion of demand for certified human-made goods and services. Think "organic" for AI — a trustmark that consumers and brands pay a premium for. You build and license the "Handmade by Human" certification standard: for products (art, craft, writing), for services (legal advice, therapy, creative direction), and for content (journalism, photography, music). You issue certifications, run audits, and license the mark. This is a standards and IP business with compounding network effects.
