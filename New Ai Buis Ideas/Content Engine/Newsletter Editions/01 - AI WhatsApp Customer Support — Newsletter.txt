THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

South Africa has over 23 million WhatsApp users — the highest penetration rate in Africa. Yet 80% of SMEs still handle customer queries manually, losing leads after hours and burning staff hours on repetitive questions. This business deploys AI-powered WhatsApp bots for South African SMEs, automating customer support, bookings, and lead capture at a fraction of the cost of a human agent. You charge a monthly retainer, require zero technical knowledge from clients, and can run the entire operation solo with the right AI stack.
