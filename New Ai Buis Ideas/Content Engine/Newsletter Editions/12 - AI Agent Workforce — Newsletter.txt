THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

AI agents are autonomous software programs that can browse the web, send emails, fill forms, analyze data, and complete multi-step tasks without human intervention. In 2025, tools like AutoGPT, CrewAI, and Anthropic's Claude Agents make it possible to deploy "virtual employees" that work 24/7 at R500–R2,000/month each (vs. R12,000–R25,000 for a human). You build, deploy, and manage AI agent workforces for mid-size SA companies — replacing or augmenting their admin, research, content, and customer service teams.
