THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The AI agent economy will follow the same pattern as the app store economy — a marketplace where specialized agents are bought, deployed, and monetized. You build the SA-first marketplace for business-grade AI agents: vetted agents across categories (marketing, finance, legal, ops), instant deployment, and performance-based pricing. Think Fiverr meets the App Store, but for autonomous AI workers. Timing: 18–36 months from now is the inflection point for agent adoption.
