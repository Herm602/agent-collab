Idea Spotlight: AI FOR ACCOUNTANTS
Phase: PHASE 1 — MAKE MONEY NOW (0-6 months) | SA Market Focus | Levinn Group

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

South Africa has 40,000+ registered accounting firms, the majority being sole practitioners or small practices with 2–10 staff. They are drowning in data entry, bank reconciliation, client onboarding, payroll processing, and SARS deadline management. You become the AI-powered back office for small accounting firms: automate their document processing, build their client communication workflows, and create AI tools that let one accountant do the work of three. Charge R8,000–R20,000/month as a white-label AI operations partner.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
WHY SOUTH AFRICA, WHY NOW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SAICA members: 28,000+ Chartered Accountants (CAs) — top decision makers.
SAIPA members: 10,000+ Professional Accountants — underserved middle market.
IAC members: 7,000+ Independent accounting firms — most price-sensitive but highest pain.
Target sweet spot: 2–8 person practice, 50–200 clients, using Xero or QuickBooks Online.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
THE REVENUE MODEL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Monthly retainer: R8,000–R20,000/month per firm based on client volume
Setup fee: R5,000–R15,000 (covers workflow building, staff training, tool integration)
Add-on: R500/month for each additional 50-client block above baseline
Year 1 target: 10 firms at avg R12,000/month = R120,000 MRR
