THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Micro-SaaS is the smartest play for a non-developer entrepreneur in 2025: use AI coding tools (Cursor, Replit, Lovable.dev) to build a tiny software product for a specific niche, charge R200–R800/month per user, and own the recurring revenue permanently. The key is extreme specificity — not "accounting software" but "invoice tracker for SA freelance photographers." You do not need to code from scratch; you use no-code/low-code + AI to build and ship in 30 days.

WHY SOUTH AFRICA, WHY NOW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Micro-SaaS is the fastest-growing segment of the software market globally (Indie Hackers 2024 report).
Average MicroConf founder earns $8,000–$25,000 MRR within 18 months.
SA SaaS market growing at 18% CAGR (Statista 2024).
PayFast processes R1.2 billion in transactions monthly — SA has a digital payment habit.
Replit, Lovable.dev, and Cursor have reduced development time by 70–80% for non-developers.

THE REVENUE MODEL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Freemium: Free plan (limited features) → Paid plan R299–R799/month
Annual discount: 2 months free for annual payment (boosts cash flow)
At 100 paying users @ R399/month = R39,900 MRR with near-zero marginal cost
At 500 users = R199,500 MRR — this is the target within 24 months
