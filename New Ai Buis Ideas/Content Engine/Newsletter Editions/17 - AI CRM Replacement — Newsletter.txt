THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CRMs fail because salespeople hate entering data. HubSpot and Salesforce are powerful, but in SA SMEs, adoption is consistently below 30% because the tools require manual data entry that disrupts the natural flow of WhatsApp-based SA sales. You build or white-label an AI-native CRM that listens to WhatsApp conversations, automatically logs contacts, tracks deal progress from chat context, summarizes every interaction, and proactively alerts salespeople on follow-up timing. No manual entry. No dashboards to maintain. The CRM does the admin; the salesperson closes deals.
