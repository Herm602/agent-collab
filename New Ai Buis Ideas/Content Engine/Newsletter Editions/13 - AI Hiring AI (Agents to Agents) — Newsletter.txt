Idea Spotlight: AI HIRING AI (AGENTS TO AGENTS)
Phase: PHASE 2 — BUILDING MOMENTUM (6-24 months) | SA Market Focus | Levinn Group

THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

As AI agents become more capable, businesses will need to hire specialized agents for specific tasks — just like hiring specialist contractors. "AI Hiring AI" is the orchestration layer: a platform where businesses define a task, an orchestrator AI evaluates available specialist agents, selects the best combination, and deploys them. You build this platform for SA enterprises, starting as a consulting practice (helping companies build their own agent orchestration systems) and evolving into a SaaS platform. This is a 12–18 month build, but the consulting revenue starts in month 1.
