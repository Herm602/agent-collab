Idea Spotlight: AI LEAD QUALIFICATION BOT

THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Every SA business with a sales team has the same problem: salespeople waste 60% of their time chasing unqualified leads. An AI lead qualification bot intercepts every inbound inquiry, asks the right questions, scores the lead, and routes only hot prospects to human salespeople. You build and deploy these bots for real estate agents, insurance brokers, car dealers, and financial advisors — niches with high lead volume and massive cost-per-acquisition. You charge per qualified lead delivered, creating a performance-based model clients love.

WHY SOUTH AFRICA, WHY NOW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Real estate: 40,000+ registered agents in SA. Top 1% earn R80,000+/month — they will pay R10,000/month to only talk to qualified buyers.
Car dealerships: Average dealer receives 300+ leads/month from AutoTrader/WeBuyCars. Qualification rate is < 15%.
Insurance brokers: FAnews reports 120,000+ FSCA-licensed brokers in SA. Per-lead cost from aggregators is R200–500.
Financial advisors (CFPs): 15,000+ in SA, average client acquisition cost is R8,000. AI drops this to R500.

THE REVENUE MODEL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Two pricing models — use whichever the client prefers:
Performance Model: R80–150 per qualified lead delivered (7+ score). No risk for client.
Retainer Model: R8,000–15,000/month for unlimited qualification up to agreed volume.
Hybrid: R5,000 setup + R60 per qualified lead. Best margin for you once volume builds.
