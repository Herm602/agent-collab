THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

As AI agents get access to company systems, data, and external services, controlling those permissions becomes critical. Just as IAM (Identity and Access Management) became essential infrastructure for cloud, "Agent Permission Management" will be essential infrastructure for the AI era. You build the enterprise software that defines, monitors, and audits what every AI agent in a company is allowed to do — what data it can read, what actions it can take, what it cannot. This is a B2B enterprise SaaS play with high switching costs and recurring revenue.
