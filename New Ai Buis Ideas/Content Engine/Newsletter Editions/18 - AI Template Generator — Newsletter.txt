THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Every business needs the same core documents repeatedly: employment contracts, supplier agreements, POPIA consent forms, company policies, proposal templates, quotation formats, and marketing briefs. In SA, a lawyer charges R5,000–R20,000 to draft each document. You build an AI-powered document generation platform specifically for the SA market — POPIA-compliant, Labour Relations Act (LRA) aligned, using South African contract law. Users input their details, AI generates the document, and they download in Word or PDF. Subscription model, massive volume, near-zero marginal cost.
