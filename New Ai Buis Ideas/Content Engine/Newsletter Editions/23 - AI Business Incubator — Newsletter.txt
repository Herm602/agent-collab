THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Rather than building one AI business, build the infrastructure that spawns many. An AI Business Incubator combines your operational expertise (Levinn Group), AI tool stack, and strategic guidance to accelerate AI-native startups in SA. You invest R100,000–R500,000 per cohort (cash + services), take 10–25% equity, and provide a shared platform of AI tools, distribution channels, and mentorship. As the SA AI ecosystem matures, your portfolio compounds. This is the Berkshire Hathaway model applied to the AI revolution.
