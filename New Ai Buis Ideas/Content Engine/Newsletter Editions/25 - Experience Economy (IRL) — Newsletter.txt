THE BIG IDEA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The paradox of AI ubiquity: the more digital life is automated, the more people crave raw, unscripted, human physical experiences. The Experience Economy is exploding globally — escape rooms, wilderness retreats, craft workshops, live music, cooking classes with famous chefs. As an AI entrepreneur, you spot this counter-trend early and build a premium IRL experience business that AI cannot replicate. In SA, this plays into the strong culture of braai, community, Ubuntu, and outdoor adventure. You build a portfolio of high-end physical experiences, use AI only for operations (booking, marketing
