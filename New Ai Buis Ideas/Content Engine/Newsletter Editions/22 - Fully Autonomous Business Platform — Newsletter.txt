The ultimate expression of the AI business revolution: a platform where an entrepreneur describes a business goal ("Build a profitable e-commerce store selling handmade SA crafts to the US market") and the AI platform autonomously builds the business — registering domains, setting up Shopify, sourcing suppliers on Etsy, writing product descriptions, running ads, handling customer service, and optimizing for profit. You are the builder and operator of this platform, taking equity or revenue share from each autonomous business launched.
