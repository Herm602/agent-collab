Steps (5)

1

Send welcome email to new hire with first day instructions, parking information, and what to bring (ID, banking details for payroll).

2

Prepare workstation: set up laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse, and ensure all software is installed (Office 365, Slack, company-specific tools).

3

Complete HR documentation: employment contract, tax forms (IRP3), UIF registration, and company policies acknowledgment.

4

Office tour: introduce team members, show emergency exits, fire extinguishers, first aid kit, kitchen area, and bathroom locations.

5

Schedule 30-60-90 day check-in meetings with manager and set initial goals and expectations for the role.
